WHO Are We?

The Subs is a Belgian electro band founded by Jeroen ‘Papillon’ De Pessemier and Wiebe ‘Tonic’ Loccufier. Originally from Ghent.In the wake of their arrival on the international scene, The Subs made several remixes for artists like Alex Gopher, The Lotterboys, Plump DJs, Telex and Cassius.
